ROB|ARCH2024 was in Toronto, CA

The city is undergoing a renaissance, in terms of construction, technology, and a thriving arts and culture scene. Toronto is home to leading academic and industrial institutions across the fields of technology, computer science, and architecture. The greater Toronto region serves as a dynamic laboratory for both locals and visitors. The city’s multicultural networks and international connections make Toronto an ideal place to host an international conference.

The unique composition of the hosting team coming together from three major Toronto institutions will allow us to not only maximise available resources for workshops and the conference, but also create precedent for cross-institutional collaboration for future conferences and events.
