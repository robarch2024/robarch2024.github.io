ROB|ARCH2024 saw eight hands-on workshops run over three days at the University of Toronto’s Daniels Faculty and Toronto Metropolitan University’s Design + Technology Lab. Overseen and organized by workshop leads Jonathon Anderson, Nicholas Hoban and Zachary Mollica, these intensive courses were hosted by an international cohort of leading researchers and practitioners who were joined by 115 participants.

Interfacing between digital and physical worlds, each of the eight workshops touched on a different domain of robotics research, picking up on both emerging and well-established pathways in robotic art, research, design, and construction.
